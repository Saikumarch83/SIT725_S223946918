Why Data Protection Matters

Personal data is at the core of our daily interactions. Whether it's banking details, medical records, or online profiles, our data is constantly being processed, stored, and shared. Protecting this data is essential to prevent misuse, identity theft, fraud, and other cyber threats.

In recent years, regulations such as the GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) have made it mandatory for organizations to protect personal data and give individuals control over how their information is used. As a result, protecting your personal data is not only a matter of privacy but a legal requirement.
